About Us

SVP High School was developed in the 19th century.
It is situated at Ahemdabad, Gujarat.
SVP was named on our lately freedom fighter Shri Sardar Patel.

SVP High School is ranked 4th within Ahemdabad.
We provide physical, mental, social Educational Knowledge to our students.
We provide the best teachers.

SVP High school is also known for its cultural activities
Arts, Dance, sports, yoga, folk-art, singing, festive celebrations.
